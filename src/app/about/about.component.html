Objective:

Accessibility and inclusion of Persons with disabilities (PwDs) in the workforce remains a challenging one on a global scale and with much more diversity in our Country's context. There have been ongoing attempts and measures to ensure the inclusion of PwDs in the workforce especially in the Government and PSUs level by the Department and other agencies working for them. This includes the Job Coach programme, an introduction to job support services, with support for application, training for competitive examination in an accessible manner, guidance to the PSUs and Department for accessible conduct of competitive examinations, counselling, and logistic support for candidates to write / attend the examination and interview thereafter. Finally, on placement, support for workplace adaptations or reasonable accommodation to sustain the job. The Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD), National Institutions like NIEPMD, collaborating with Government and Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) support with such job coach programme and assist Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) especially D andamp; E category, the most vulnerable group with either cognitive, learning, severe mobility, behaviour management, socialisation, or multiple such difficulties.
